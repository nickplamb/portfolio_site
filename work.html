My Work

Chicken Coop Data Logger

An Arduino project to log the temperature and humidity inside and outside my chicken coop. It is solar powered and POSTs the data to a simple PHP script and MySQL database on a home server. The script will also send the data to Thingspeak and will send me a text message if the door to the coop is open and a specified time.

Rising Sun Farms data site

A Laravel based website I created to allow me to submit data about my chickens, their egg production, and other animals we have, past and present. It stores data such as name, breed, and birth/hatch date of the animals as well as egg data such as the day it was layed and it weight
